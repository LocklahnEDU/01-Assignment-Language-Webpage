Careers in Web and User Experience


What is UX and UI?

UX

User experience jobs often involve more than just making the product usable and often focuses on things such as fun and efficency of the product for the user

UI

User ineterface focuses on making the product or interface work and perform its task

Careers in UX and UI

    UX researcher: Conducts qualitative and quantitative research on the users of a product.
    UX engineer: Codes the interfaces of websites and apps using UX principles.
    UX writer: Writes copy for digital products, such as websites and apps.
    Information architect: Organizes and structures digital content to make it easy to find and use.
    UI designer: Designs user interfaces, or what humans interact with on screen.

Other Information

The average salary for UX designers in the US is $95,572 but can range anywhere between $75,000 to $113,368 and is considered among the best paying jobs in the country.

As the feild of UX has evolved we have seen an increase in specialized UX jobs such as researcher or engineer and the US Bureau of Labor Statistics predicteds that UX careers will only continue to grow.
